Fairyland is a magical island in the middle of the ocean, filled with mountains, rivers, palm trees, white sand beaches, and glowing waters.

At night, the ocean sparkles like stars, and the air is filled with the sound of waves, birds, and ritual dances that protect the Island. Fairyland is a peaceful and enchanted place where magic is part of everyday life.
